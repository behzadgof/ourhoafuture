# Objective

Present the garage repair project in a stronger accountability format so residents can clearly see the documented spending and evaluate stewardship concerns.

The page should emphasize:

- documented money amounts
- documented scope limitations

- Statements must be tied to documented records shown on the page.
- Language may express serious concern about stewardship and fiscal responsibility.
- Do not assert legal claims or unverified allegations.
