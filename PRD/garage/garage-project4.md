# Messaging and Tone Rules

The page should use an **evidence-led concern** style:

- factual
- document-based
- informational
- strong but non-defamatory

Allowed framing examples:

- "The documented cost-to-scope contrast raises concerns about project management efficiency."
- "Residents may reasonably question whether execution was proportionate to the documented scope."

Not allowed:

- legal allegations
- claims of fraud, theft, or intentional misconduct
- statements presented as fact without supporting documentation
