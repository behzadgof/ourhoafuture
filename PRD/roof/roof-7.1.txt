In the Contractor Proposal

Advanced Roofing's detailed proposal lists totals of approximately:

$1,719,270 (Soprema roofing system with bond)

Because these documents present pricing in different formats, the totals may not be directly comparable without additional explanation.

Contractor	Mobilization

Mobilization typically refers to the costs required to start a construction project. These may include:

equipment delivery

dumpsters and lifts

staging areas

temporary protection

project setup logistics

supervision and safety preparation

Contractors sometimes structure these costs differently depending on their proposal format.

Typical Construction Project Process

Roof replacement projects normally follow this process:

The engineer prepares project specifications

Contractors submit proposals (bids)

The engineer prepares a bid comparison sheet

The board selects a contractor

A construction contract is negotiated and signed

Work is scheduled and begins

The documents currently posted appear to correspond to the proposal and bid comparison stage of this process.
