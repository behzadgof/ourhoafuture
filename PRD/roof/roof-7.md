# Advanced Roofing Pricing – Why Two Different Numbers Appear

Residents may notice that two different price ranges appear in the documents for **Advanced Roofing**.

These numbers come from **two different types of documents**.

## 1. Engineer Bid Comparison Sheet

The engineer’s bid comparison sheet lists the following total for Advanced Roofing:

**Approximately $999,153**

This document summarizes the bids submitted by each contractor so they can be compared side-by-side.

## 2. Advanced Roofing Detailed Proposal

The actual proposal submitted by Advanced Roofing lists significantly higher totals depending on the roofing system selected:

- **$1,719,270 (Soprema roofing system with bond)**
- **$1,778,603 (TPO roofing system with bond)**

## Why These Numbers May Be Different

Different totals can appear when documents are presented in different formats. Possible reasons include:

- different cost categories being grouped together
- bond costs being included or excluded
- optional work or alternates being treated differently
- mobilization or general conditions being structured differently

Because the engineer’s bid comparison sheet and the contractor proposal present pricing in different formats, the totals may not be directly comparable without additional explanation.

## Why Clarification Would Be Helpful

Providing a reconciliation between the engineer’s bid comparison sheet and the contractor’s detailed proposal could help residents understand:

- how the **$999K figure** was calculated
- how it relates to the **$1.7M proposal**
- which totals are being used for budgeting and decision making
