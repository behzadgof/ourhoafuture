Your instinct is exactly right: if residents have to dig to find the financial story, most of them never will. The homepage needs to visually punch them in the face with the key numbers while still keeping your profile and campaign message.

After reviewing your homepage HTML, the problem is clear:

Your homepage currently opens with mission language and navigation, not the financial reality. The key financial numbers only appear deeper in the site. 

index

Your financial dashboard itself is well designed with cards and graphs, but it lives on a separate page, so the impact is lost unless someone clicks into it. 

financial

The solution is not moving the financial page.
The solution is creating a “Financial Shock Section” on the homepage that links into the deeper dashboard.

Recommended Homepage Strategy

The homepage should follow this order:

1️⃣ Hero Message (keep it)

Your current hero message is good.

But immediately after it, insert:

2️⃣ Financial Reality Section (NEW)

This becomes the visual punch.

Example:

-----------------------------------
TURNBERRY 2025 FINANCIAL SNAPSHOT
-----------------------------------

Operating Deficit
-$177,539

Administrative Overrun
+$217,462

Legal Spending
$208,857

Utilities Over Budget
+$76,328

These should appear as large red cards.

Residents should see this before they scroll.
