# Project Overview

In 2025, structural repair work was performed in the parking garage structure at Turnberry on the Green.

The repairs focused on a localized section of the parking garage concrete structure.  
The work involved repairing portions of the **double-tee concrete slab system** and temporarily supporting the structure while repairs were completed.

Temporary steel support posts (structural shoring) were installed to stabilize the slab during the repair process.

---

# Scope of Work

Based on the engineering proposal and contractor agreements, the scope of the project included:

- Structural inspection of the damaged garage area
- Review of existing structural drawings
- Engineering evaluation of the damage
- Development of repair specifications
- Installation of temporary structural shoring
- Repair of double-tee flange areas
- Slab jacking / leveling work
- Welding and reinforcement of structural connection points
- Sealant and joint repairs
- Follow-up engineering inspections during the repair process

The work was limited to a **specific section of the garage structure**.

---
