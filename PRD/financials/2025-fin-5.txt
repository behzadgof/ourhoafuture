The 5 Financial Charts Every Resident Should See
Why These Charts Matter

Financial statements are often difficult to understand.
These five charts simplify the 2025 financial reports so residents can clearly see:

how expenses changed

where money was spent

why the operating deficit occurred

how cash and reserves moved during the year

All figures are derived from the monthly financial statements issued by the association.

What These Charts Show

From the financial statements:

1️⃣ The association ran an operating deficit in 2025.
2️⃣ Administrative spending exceeded budget by over $217,000.
3️⃣ Legal expenses doubled the annual budget.
4️⃣ Utilities and repairs also exceeded projections.
5️⃣ Cash and reserve balances increased despite the deficit.

Why Financial Transparency Matters

Strong financial management helps ensure:

stable assessments

proper maintenance of the property

responsible use of community funds

confidence among residents

Clear financial reporting allows all residents to better understand the financial health of the association.
