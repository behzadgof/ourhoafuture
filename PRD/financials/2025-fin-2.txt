2025 Financial Timeline & Operational Trends
Purpose

This page provides a visual timeline and analysis of the association’s financial activity during 2025 based on the monthly financial statements provided by management.

The goal is to help residents understand:

how operational expenses evolved

where budget overruns occurred

how cash balances changed during the year

whether reserves were used to support operations

All figures are derived from the monthly financial statements issued by the association.

Financial Warning Indicators

Several issues appear in the financial statements:

1. Missing Reconciliations

Early statements referenced missing bank reconciliations.

2. Forensic Audit

Reports indicate financials were under forensic review.

3. Large Receivable Fluctuations

Receivable balances changed significantly during the year.

4. Administrative Overspending

Administrative spending exceeded budget by more than 113%.

5. Legal Cost Escalation

Legal expenses exceeded budget by over $108,000.

Questions Residents May Ask

Why did administrative expenses more than double the budget?

What caused legal costs to exceed $200,000?

Why were financial statements under forensic audit?

What explains large swings in receivables and internal transfers?

How will future budgets prevent operational deficits?

Conclusion

The 2025 financial statements indicate:

Operations ran a deficit

Expenses exceeded budget in several categories

Administrative and legal costs were primary drivers

Total cash and reserves increased during the year

The key issue is therefore operational spending discipline rather than reserve depletion.
