Mobilization Costs Listed in the Bid Documents

Mobilization may be structured differently by contractor
ContractorMobilization
CARE Construction$15,000
Advanced Roofing$548,665
JK Inc$41,500

Mobilization generally refers to the costs required to prepare a project before work begins.

These costs can include:

  • equipment delivery
  • dumpsters and lifts
  • staging areas
  • temporary protection
  • project setup logistics
  • supervision and safety preparation

Contractors sometimes organize these costs differently in their proposals.

Typical Construction Project Process

Where these documents appear to fit in a normal project workflow
  1. The engineer prepares project specifications
  2. Contractors submit proposals (bids)
  3. The engineer prepares a bid comparison summary
  4. The board reviews the proposals and selects a contractor
  5. A construction contract is negotiated and signed
  6. Work is scheduled and begins

The documents currently posted appear to correspond to the proposal and bid comparison stage of the process.
