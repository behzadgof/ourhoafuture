Clarifications That Improve Comparability

Neutral questions for owners and management
  1. Which Advanced Roofing total is used for budgeting: bid-tab value or detailed proposal value?
  2. Are all contractors pricing the same final scope and quantity takeoff?
  3. What costs are included within each contractor's mobilization category?
  4. Are bond, electrical work, and alternates treated consistently across all proposals?
  5. Has an engineer reconciliation been prepared between bid-tab summary and detailed proposals?

Providing a reconciliation between bid-tab and proposal totals would improve comparability for all bidders and make the pricing basis clearer for residents.
