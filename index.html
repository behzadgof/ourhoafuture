Why This Site Exists

Our community has experienced increased assessments, special insurance assessments, and major projects requiring clearer cost transparency.

This site was created to help residents better understand budgets, project costs, and how association funds are being used through summary analysis and supporting information.

The site provides:

  • project cost breakdowns
  • budget analysis
  • contract summaries
  • visual documentation of major projects
