Parking Garage Structural Repair Project (2025)

Summary of repair contracts and project documentation

This page provides a summary of the parking garage structural repair project based on contracts and documents available to residents.

Project Overview

In 2025, structural repair work was performed in the second-floor parking structure.

The work involved repairs to the double-tee concrete structure, including flange repair and slab jacking. Temporary structural shoring was installed to support the slab during the repair process.

The project was executed under two contracts with Age of Empire Construction.
